About Me

Greetings! I’m Shreesha, a passionate Designer & Developer navigating the intersection of creativity and functionality. I craft intuitive user experiences by blending design aesthetics with seamless code, ensuring that every element serves a purpose.

My journey includes an enriching internship at Open Weaver, where I designed user-friendly templates and worked on exciting client projects. Prior to that, I spent two years at Scribbles, creating functional yet stylish school uniforms—an experience that honed my ability to balance usability with aesthetics.

I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, PostgreSQL, React.js, Figma, and Illustrator, bridging the gap between design and development. Constantly evolving, I aim to build products that are both visually engaging and technically robust.
